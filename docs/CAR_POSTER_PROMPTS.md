# Iranian combat-racing car poster prompts

These prompts are for original game key art. Each source image is a **vehicle identity reference only**: preserve the car's recognizable body shape, armor, weapon placement, wear, and main colors, but rebuild the setting, lighting, and composition as new artwork. The posters deliberately avoid copied branding, existing racing-game layouts, political symbols, and readable Persian text. Iranian influence comes through landscape, architecture, geometric craft, light, and atmosphere.

## Shared art direction

- Portrait 2:3 premium game key-art poster, full bleed.
- The vehicle dominates the lower two-thirds in a low front three-quarter action view, with the full silhouette readable and wheels grounded.
- Cinematic industrial realism with tactile scratched steel, dust, oil, heat haze, sparks, and restrained motion blur.
- Near-black night palette, amber action light, and one car-specific accent color.
- Iranian influence should feel integrated and respectful: real landscape and architectural cues, Persian geometric pattern language, and abstract calligraphic energy only.
- No people, national flags, government or religious emblems, real brands, copied franchise imagery, watermark, border, UI, title, or readable text. Leave a calm dark band at the top for game-added typography.
